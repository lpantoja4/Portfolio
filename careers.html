Additional Information

MIT
The tuition price at MIT about $59,750 and to apply, you should aim for the 75th percentile, with a 1570 SAT or a 36 ACT. You should also have a 4.17 GPA or higher.
UIUC
The tuition price at UIUC is about $15,442. To apply, you require a GPA of 3.83, your transcript should show mostly A's, have taken several AP or IB classes to show that you can handle academics at a college level.
Career
Someone in the position of front end development is responsible for making the user interface and experience of a website. The average salary of a front web developer is around $107,824. Most front-end development careers require a bachelor's degree in computer science or another related field and also need to have a strong proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
